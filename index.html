Analytics & Growth

Daily Active Users (DAU): We surged to a peak of nearly 7,000 concurrent DAU during our major release window and currently average over 2,000. PC users consistently make up our largest and most engaged demographic.

Revenue Growth: The game has generated over 610,000 Robux in total revenue over this period, with massive spikes perfectly correlating with our peak player counts in late March and early April.

Engagement: Players spend an average of 18.3 minutes per session. This high playtime remains consistent across all platforms, proving that our complex building systems successfully keep players engaged.

Monetization (ARPPU): Our Average Revenue Per Paying User remains exceptionally strong at 378.75 Robux, showing that our community finds high value in the premium features and game passes we've implemented.
